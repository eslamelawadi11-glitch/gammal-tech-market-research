# 🏥 Market Research: Health Apps Pain Points (Sehhaty Case Study)

This research identifies the core frustrations of health app users in the region based on **50 real negative reviews** collected between late 2025 and early 2026. This data serves as a foundation for building a superior user experience for **healthcare.gammal.tech**.

---

## 🔍 Top 3 User Pain Points

### 1. The "Immediate" Consultation Gap (Telemedicine Failure)
Users report extreme delays in the "Immediate Consultation" feature. Despite being marketed as "immediate," patients often wait for hours or even days without a response, leading to a loss of trust in the digital healthcare system.
> **Evidence:** *"لي ساعة ونص منتظر دكتور ولا دخل... يا ما يدخل دكتور يا أطباء مشغولون بس"* (Dec 4, 2025)
> **Evidence:** *"مرة انتظرت أكثر من 5 ساعات واثر الطبيب قد قفل وراح والتطبيق لازال يدور"* (Dec 24, 2025)

### 2. Technical Instability & Regression (Update Issues)
A significant number of users reported that the app became "broken" after recent updates in early 2026. This indicates a lack of thorough regression testing before deployment.
> **Evidence:** *"التطبيق كان قبل التحديث الأخير واللي قبله شغال الحين لا يعمل بدخل ويطلع تاني تلقائي"* (Feb 5, 2026)
> **Evidence:** *"صار يتأخر والتحديث سيئ، كان أول يعمل بشكل ممتاز وبدون تأخير"* (Jan 4, 2026)

### 3. Data Persistence & Cache Bugs (UX Friction)
A recurring technical bug prevents users from seeing new medical reports or leaves of absence unless they **delete and reinstall** the app—a major friction point for non-technical users.
> **Evidence:** *"ما يفتح الاجازة او المشهد الجديد حتى اختيارها يفتح اخر اجازة فتحتها اضطر احذف التطبيق وارجع اثبته"* (Feb 2, 2026)
