# 👥 User Interviews Summary

This document summarizes the findings from 1-on-1 interviews with potential users for **healthcare.gammal.tech**. The goal is to understand real-world health habits and the demand for preventive features.

---

## 👤 Interview #1: Ashraf Emad
**Profile:** Active individual, focus on fitness and daily exercise.
**Date:** March 2026

### 📝 Key Insights:

| Question | User Response |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Current Health Habits** | Drinks water frequently, runs daily, and goes to the gym regularly. |
| **Preventive Actions** | Performs a **full medical check-up every 6 months** to stay ahead of any issues. |
| **App Usage** | Currently **does not use** any health apps. |
| **Early Warning System** | Very enthusiastic about a feature that sends **daily early warnings** about potential health issues. |
| **Feature Requests** | Wants a "one-stop-shop" app that combines sports, health, and constant notifications. |

### 💡 Analyst's Note:
Ashraf represents a "Highly Disciplined" user segment. He is already invested in prevention (6-month checkups), but lacks a digital tool that centralizes his data. The **"Daily Early Warning"** is the primary "Hook" that would convert him into a daily user of healthcare.gammal.tech.

## 👤 Interview #2: Dr. Yasmin Mohamed
**Profile:** Healthcare professional (Doctor).
**Date:** March 2026

### 📝 Key Insights:

| Question | User Response |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Current Health Habits** | Focuses on nutrition (Protein/Calcium), drinks ~3 liters of water daily, and attends the gym. |
| **Preventive Actions** | Strict personal hygiene protocols and social distancing from infected individuals to avoid contagion. |
| **App Usage** | Currently **does not use** any health apps. |
| **Early Warning System** | Highly recommends it, especially for **chronic patients** (Diabetes/Hypertension) to remind them of medication or alert them to risks. |
| **Feature Requests** | Water intake reminders, specialized nutritional plans, and access to specialized doctors for quick consultations. |
| **Search Habits** | When feeling unwell, she searches to understand the cause or symptoms of her condition. |

### 💡 Analyst's Note:
Dr. Yasmin provides the perspective of a "Medical Expert" user. Her focus is on **Precision** (specialized nutrition and doctors) and **Condition Management**. Her endorsement of the "Early Warning System" for chronic diseases validates the clinical value of healthcare.gammal.tech beyond just general fitness.
